                            MORNING BUSINESS

  The PRESIDENT pro tempore. Under the previous order, there will now 
be a period for the transaction of morning business for up to 60 
minutes, with the first half of the time under the control of the 
majority leader and the second half of the time under the control of 
the Democratic leader.
