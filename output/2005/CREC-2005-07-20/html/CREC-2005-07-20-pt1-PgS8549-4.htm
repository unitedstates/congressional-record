           MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE RECEIVED DURING ADJOURNMENT

                                 ______
                                 

                          ENROLLED BILL SIGNED

  Under the authority of the order of January 4, 2005, the Secretary of 
the Senate, on July 19, 2005, received a message from the House of 
Representatives announcing that the Speaker has signed the following 
enrolled bill:

       H.R. 3332. An act to provide an extension of highway, 
     highway safety, motor carrier safety, transit, and other 
     programs funded out of the Highway Trust Fund pending 
     enactment of a law reauthorizing the Transportation Equity 
     Act for the 21st century.

  Under the authority of the order of July 19, 2005, the enrolled bill 
was signed on July 19, 2005, during the adjournment of the Senate, by 
the Majority Leader (Mr. Frist).
