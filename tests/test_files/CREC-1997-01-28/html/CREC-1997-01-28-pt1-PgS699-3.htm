                                 RECESS

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Under the previous order, the hour of 12:30
having arrived, the Senate will stand in recess until the hour of 2:15
p.m.
  Thereupon, the Senate, at 12:30 p.m., recessed until 2:15 p.m.;
whereupon, the Senate reassembled when called to order by the Presiding
Officer (Mr. Enzi).
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The Senator from Virginia is recognized.
