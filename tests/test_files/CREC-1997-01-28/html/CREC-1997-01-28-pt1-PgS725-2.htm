                    EXECUTIVE REPORTS OF COMMITTEES

  The following executive reports of committees were submitted:

       By Mr. D'AMATO, from the Committee on Banking, Housing, and
     Urban Affairs:
       Alan M. Hantman, of New Jersey, to be Architect of the
     Capitol for the term of 10 years.
       By Mr. WARNER, from the Committee on Rules and
     Administration:
       Andrew M. Cuomo, of New York, to be Secretary of Housing
     and Urban Development.

  (The above nominations were reported with the recommendation that
they be confirmed, subject to the nominees' commitment to respond to
requests to appear and testify before any duly constituted committee of
the Senate.)
